The IRS has created two paths to 501(c)3 tax-exempt status based on expected donations:

1. Organizations confident they will raise more than $50,000 in the current tax year or in any of the following two tax years qualify for our Enterprise Plan.

2. Organizations with expected donations below $50,000/year, or without a clear path to exceed that amount, qualify for our Express Plan.

About 82% of our customers qualify for the Streamlined IRS Application for Tax Exempt Status, while the other 18% must use the Long-Form 1023 Process (see Enterprise and Evolved).
